Our primary objective is to organize, record and present the information required to obtain your Australian provisional licence. It is made available as a service to you through any software offered by us.

Here we will describe:

  • What Information we collect and why we collect it
  • How we use information we collect
  • Information we share
  • Information security
  • Changes

Information we collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our service to you.

  • Account. We require you to sign up for a Mylogbook Account to use our service. When you do, we’ll ask for personal information like your name, email address and birthdate. This information helps us improve and personalize your user experience. Your email is used to: identify you, notify you of events or updates, take advantage of security features and to send you resources.
  • Local. While using our service, various data you provide is stored locally on your device. This includes: the date you received your learner’s permit, the current state you reside in and information you provide about your progression towards your provisional licence. The exact information you provide will vary slightly depending on the state you reside in. This data is collected to aid us in providing our service to the best of our ability and as accurately as possible, ultimately providing a better user experience.
  • Service. Our service allows you to create cars, supervisors and logs. It includes your car registration numbers and supervisor names. A log is a recording of a trip taken as a learner driver from a starting point to a final destination. Information about your trip is recorded with data you provide and information we collect in the background. We collect data with the global positioning system (GPS) technology on your phone and we may use third-party services to obtain more information as required. This includes: start and end time, your cars odometer, distance travelled, weather, traffic, road types, light conditions, start and end geographic coordinates, start and end locations, time zone, the car you used and the supervisor instructing you. This information is stored in a local database and synced to our database online. This information is collected solely to provide and improve our service.
  • Usage. We may collect information on how you use the service and your experience using it. This includes actions you take in your account, system activity, crashes and errors. We may also collect device-specific information such as the unique device identifier (UID), operating system version and hardware model. This information is to help us improve our service.

How we use information we collect

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, protect and improve our service. Nothing more.

Information we share

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals outside of Mylogbook unless:

  • It meets any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.
  • It can detect, prevent or address security or technical issues.
  • To protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of Mylogbook, our users or the public as required or permitted by law.

Information security

We work hard to protect our users from unauthorized access, unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold. This includes:

  • Encrypting our service using SSL.
  • Storing sensitive information in encrypted storage.
  • Reviewing our information collection, storage and processing practices.

Changes

Our privacy policy may change from time to time. However, we will not reduce your rights under this privacy policy without your consent. We will post any privacy policy changes here and if the changes are significant, we will provide an email notification.
